Protest started taking place by the peasants / ryots supported by the zamindars.Ryots refused to pay the rent to the planers, and attacked Indigo factories armed with sword and spears, bows and arrows. Women turned up to fight with pots, pans and kitchen

implements.Gomasthas the agents of planters who came to collect the rent were beaten up.The indigo ryots felt that they had the support of the local zamindars and village headmen in their rebellion against the planters.In 1859 Lieutenant Governor toured the regions seeing the conditions of ryots. After it the Indigo Commission was constituted by the government which accepted the fault of the planters and asked them to stop the cultivation.Planters eventually moved out of the cities.
