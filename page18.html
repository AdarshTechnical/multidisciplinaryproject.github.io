The 1857 Revolt

Policies of East India Company and the force ship of the company ignited the spark into a big revolt of 1857.

1.The britishers wanted quick profits and heavy taxes forced farmers to abandon their field.

2.The British policies ruined the expert artisans and their business.

3.The British prospered on the company loot while Indian industries began die.

4.Taxes continued to ruined the Indians peasants, and having lost their lands under new land rules, became desperate.

5.Santhals were the first to begin the journey to fight for their rights and this began revolt of 1857.
